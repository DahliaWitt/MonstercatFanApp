Atmozfears

Atmozfears is an alias of TVDS, aka Tim van de Stadt, a 22-year old producer based in Enschede, Netherlands. Under this alias, he produces hardstyle. Originally a trio, consisting of Tim van de Stadt (TVDS), Michael Jessen, and Kevin Keiser, at the end of 2012, Michael Jessen left the band. He is not currently producing. On July 1, 2013, Kevin Keiser left to form his own musical project, InfRno, leaving TVDS as the sole member of Atmozfears. Although they released one song on Monstercat, Atmozfears is signed to Scantraxx, and releases almost all his music there. Atmozfears is very popular in the hardstyle community, with his Soundcloud page having over 12,000 more followers than the TVDS page.
